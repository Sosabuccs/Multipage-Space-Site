Facts

Here are some facts about the solar system

  • The Solar System is made up of the Sun and everything that orbits, or travels around, the Sun – the eight planets. Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus.
  • The Solar System is 4.6 billion years old. That’s over 100 million Earths put together
  • The Solar System is a fascinating place full of wonders waiting to be discovered.
  • The Solar System is a very large place. It is about 4.6 billion miles across and about 4.6 billion miles high.
  • The Solar System consists of the Sun and everything that orbits it, including the eight planets, Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.
